# healthtalk.org

Health Talk

Healthy minds, healthy lives


Overview
Mental health is a critical component of overall wellness, influencing how individuals think, feel, and interact with the world.  
Health Talk is a platform dedicated to promoting awareness, education, and dialogue around mental health. By providing reliable information and encouraging open discussions, we aim to reduce stigma and empower individuals to seek help when needed.


Mission
At Health Talk, we believe true wellness begins with the mind.  
Our mission is to:
- Break the silence around mental health.
- Foster open conversations.
- Offer trusted resources.
- Build supportive communities.

We envision a world where:
- Seeking help is seen as strength.
- Resilience is celebrated.
- Every individual has access to compassionate care.


Features
- Awareness Programs – Learn about mental health and its impact on everyday life.
- Support Resources – Access guidance and trusted information.
- Community Engagement – Join discussions and connect with others.
- Educational Content – Videos, articles, and tools to promote understanding.


Getting Started
1. Clone the repository:
   git clone https://github.com/your-username/health-talk.git

2. Navigate to the project folder:
   cd health-talk

3. Open index.html in your browser to explore the site locally.




Usage
- Visit the homepage to explore resources and programs.
- Navigate through sections like About, Programs, Support, and Videos.
- Engage with community discussions and share your experiences.
